TESTIMONY MEETING
During a Testimony Meeting, each Member of our Fellowship is invited ( but not required ) to take a few minutes of meeting time to share with the congregation that which weighs on their Mind, at that time, concerning our Faith.
There is no strict order of turn succession; Members participate via first-come, first-availability while the blocked-out time for the Meeting is still available.
Our Fellowship Consul ( or, in their absence, their designated representative ) always presides over each Meeting ‚Äì opening with official news and with their own thoughts; and closing the meeting, at-time, similarly ‚Äì in order to facilitate the Faith.
Please remember to remain quiet during the entirety of any Meeting ‚Äì reflecting upon your own views-shared as well as each other speaker's thoughts ‚Äì so that the congregation, as a whole, feels best respected and so that individuals feel heard.
( Note that Testimony Meetings aim to begin on time; and they may run long depending on the group spirit and related wants of the congregation. )
